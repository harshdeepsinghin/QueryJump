About

Features

✨

Hotkey-First Search

Select any text on a webpage and press Alt+S to instantly search it — no menus, no clicks, no friction.

Multiple Search Engines

Choose between Google, DuckDuckGo, or define your own custom search URL with {query} support.

Privacy-First

Zero tracking, zero analytics, zero data collection. Your searches stay between you and your search engine.

Cross-Browser

Works seamlessly on Chrome, Brave, Edge, and Firefox. One extension, every Chromium & Gecko browser.

Why QueryJump?

💡
QueryJump was built for one reason: searching selected text should be instant. No right-click menus, no copy-paste, no switching tabs to type a query.

Select text. Press a key. Done. That's the entire workflow.
Minimal. Fast. Private. Keyboard-first.
